The dataset consists of several CSV files related to Harry Potter movies. Here's a breakdown of the files:

Chapters.csv: Likely contains information about chapters within the movies.
Characters.csv: Information on characters from the Harry Potter movies.
Data_Dictionary.csv: Describes the structure and meaning of each column in the other files.
Dialogue.csv: Contains dialogues spoken by characters in the movies.
LICENSE: Licensing information for the dataset.
Movies.csv: General information about the movies, possibly including release dates, directors, etc.
Places.csv: Locations mentioned or visited in the movies.
Spells.csv: Information on spells used in the movies.
